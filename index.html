Why ROCKos?

ROCKos has been developed to provide a stable and comfortable environment for everything from daily use to software development workflows.

Powered by Ubuntu

With the Ubuntu base—the world's most popular Linux distribution—you get a massive package repository, strong community support, and unshakable stability.

High Security

Ubuntu's advanced security infrastructure and regular updates protect your data and digital assets at the highest level on ROCKos.

Performance Focused

With its lightweight structure stripped of unnecessary background services, it delivers high speed and smooth transitions even on low-spec hardware.

Dual Architecture

Two separate ISO images optimized for both standard 64-bit (x64) computers and next-generation ARM64-based devices (including Apple Silicon virtualizations).
